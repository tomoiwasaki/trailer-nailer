Welcome to Trailer Nailer!
The purpose of this game is to test your movie-knowledge skills to the limits!
Try to guess as many movies as you can before time is up!
Currently only 5 genres are supported, but our movie database will be expanded shortly.
In the future, we also intend to implement a "profile" module that allows you to keep track of your process.
If you liked this game please share it with your friends.
The game was developed by Minh D. Nguyen, Huynh Tran, Tomo Iwasaki, Arpan Gurung, and Georgios Frantzis Phylactopoulos in 2017.
